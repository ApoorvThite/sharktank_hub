## âš–ï¸ DEAL FAIRNESS INDEX

### Methodology

Composite score (0-100) based on three components:

1. **Valuation Reasonableness (40%)**
   - Deviation from industry median
   - 100 points for below market, 30 points for overvalued

2. **Equity Ask Fairness (30%)**
   - Appropriate for revenue stage
   - Penalty for asking too much or too little

3. **Deal Terms Complexity (30%)**
   - Penalty for debt (-20 points)
   - Penalty for royalty (-15 points)

### Distribution

| Fairness Category | Score Range | Count | % of Total | Success Rate |
|-------------------|-------------|-------|------------|--------------|
| **Highly Fair** | 80-100 | 377 | 53.7% | 63.9% |
| **Fair** | 60-80 | 267 | 38.0% | 70.0% |
| **Moderate** | 40-60 | 58 | 8.3% | 63.8% |
| **Unfair** | 0-40 | 0 | 0.0% | N/A |

### Key Findings

1. **Most Deals are Fair**
   - 91.7% score "Fair" or better
   - Average fairness score: 82.4/100
   - No deals scored as "Unfair"

2. **Fairness Correlates with Success**
   - "Fair" category: 70.0% success rate (highest)
   - "Highly Fair": 63.9% success rate
   - "Moderate": 63.8% success rate

3. **Why "Fair" Outperforms "Highly Fair"**
   - "Highly Fair" may be undervalued (red flag)
   - "Fair" shows confidence without greed
   - Sharks prefer balanced, realistic asks

4. **Complex Terms Reduce Fairness**
   - Debt reduces score by 20 points
   - Royalty reduces score by 15 points
   - Simple equity deals score highest

## ðŸ’¡ STRATEGIC RECOMMENDATIONS

### For Founders

1. **Price at or Below Market**
   - 67% success rate for below-market pricing
   - Avoid overvaluation (even if justified)
   - Leave room for negotiation

2. **Expect Significant Dilution**
   - Plan for 2-3x equity ask
   - Pre-revenue: expect 10-15% final equity
   - Revenue-generating: expect 5-8% final equity

3. **Optimize Fairness Score**
   - Keep terms simple (equity only)
   - Avoid debt and royalty if possible
   - Match equity ask to revenue stage

4. **Industry-Specific Strategies**
   - **Tech:** Emphasize scalability, IP
   - **Medical:** Highlight regulatory approvals, patents
   - **Food:** Focus on margins, distribution
   - **Fashion:** Show brand strength, repeat customers

### For Investors

1. **Use Industry Benchmarks**
   - Don't overpay relative to industry median
   - Adjust for margins, team, IP
   - Consider stage and traction

2. **Watch for Red Flags**
   - Significantly undervalued (may be desperate)
   - Significantly overvalued (unrealistic)
   - Complex terms (debt + equity + royalty)

3. **Negotiate Strategically**
   - Counter at 50-60% of asked valuation
   - Ask for 2-3x equity
   - Keep terms simple

4. **Industry-Specific Considerations**
   - **Tech:** Pay premium for scalability
   - **Medical:** Verify IP and regulatory status
   - **Food:** Check unit economics and margins
   - **Fashion:** Assess brand moat and retention
